Premise:

The Long Haul is a month long you ship we ship(YSWS), where you are only able to submit one project throughout the ENTIRE time period.

What's the longest amount of time you spent working on a project? A week? Two? How much polish abd functionality were you able to put in it?

Well, with The Long Haul, we're aiming to improve on that! Spend one month building one super duper awesome mega project, submit it, and get prizes!

What do I do?
Make a project! Wanted to make an app that sorts out all of your pictures? Want to make an alarm that forces you to solve math problems to turn off? ANYTHING GOES!(within reason :p)

How do?
That depnds! What kind of project do you want to make? It can be hardware, software, cad-ware - anything essentially! (as long as it fits the submission guidelines!) 

What counts?
The specifics are to be decided! As long as we can verify the amount of time you used, you can make anything! Use lapse(https://lapse.hackclub.com/) or hackatime(https://hackatime.hackclub.com/) to track time.

What do I get?

FAQ: 
q: What is ysws?
a: A You Ship We Ship(YSWS) is a program where you have you take time and create some sort of project and ship it for the world to see! Then, we'll ship something to you(stickers, food, and more!).
Q: Who is eligible?
a: Anybody ages 13 through 18 are eligible to participate!
q: Is this legit?
a: Yes! hack club is the world's largest community of teenage makers, and a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. we've hosted programs like high seas(https://highseas.hackclub.com/) and summer of making(https://summer.hackclub.com/) which gave out prizes for building all sorts of projects. we're supported by donations from companies like GitHub and AMD! Check them out here!(hyperlink)
q: How does the shop work?
a: Depending the amount of time you spent, the number of pit spots you hit up, along with the quality of a project, you'll unlock various tiers of the camper shop! The more time you spend improving your project's quality, you'll get access to a wider and cheaper range of items to get from the shop compared to other projects!

a: No problem! Join the slack faq channel(hyperlink), or DM @Iamalive(hyperlink) on the slack!
